Auf Etwas is a production house partnering with cultural institutions, artists, advertising agencies, and established brands. The focus is on projects that prioritize innovation and experimentation. Creative productions that foster inclusivity, bring about social impact, and provide a platform for diverse voices. Auf Etwas centers around digital art – culture: multi-media performances - creative productions and the evolution of sound.

Eva Mikelatou is a creative & performance producer from Athens who has called Berlin home since 2015. Within the journey into the culture, music, and film industries, the primary objective is to leverage her background and studies in Psychology to promote – empower underrepresented groups and communities while pushing the boundaries.
